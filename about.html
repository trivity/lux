Meet Our Founder

Katherine Monroe

Founder & Lead Aesthetician

When I opened the doors to K | Luxe Wellness, I had a vision: to create a space where luxury and wellness coexist in perfect harmony. A place where every client feels seen, heard, and genuinely cared for.

Our Journey

The K | Luxe Story

K | Luxe Wellness was born from a simple belief: that everyone deserves access to elevated wellness experiences. What started as a dream in 2020 became a reality in 2021, when we opened our doors in Aurora, Colorado.

From day one, our mission has been clear—to create a sanctuary where clinical excellence meets luxurious comfort. Where cutting-edge treatments are delivered with warmth and intention. Where you're not just another appointment, but a valued member of our wellness family.

Today, K | Luxe has grown into a trusted destination for those seeking transformative results in an environment that feels like home. But our core values remain unchanged: intentionality, elevation, and empowerment guide everything we do.
